HOWDY!

I'm Anne, but my friends call me "Annie". I am a designer, from the Lone Star state. Since graduating The University of Texas, I moved up to Big Apple to complete my Masters in Communications Design, with an emphasis in Digital Design, from the Pratt Institute.


I am an award winning designer. I specialize in interactive design, graphic design, & illustration. My approach to design is simple & accessible. I aim to create work that is fun, and doesn't take itself too seriously.


I accept freelance clients, local & not. I truly enjoy what I do & I hope you do too! Check out my credentials below. Like what you see? Drop me a line, let's make beautiful work together!
